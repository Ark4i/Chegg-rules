Rules Overview

  • Core Rule: Standard Chegg rules apply. The Villager is replaced by a Crown (movement unchanged).
  • Legend (L): Max 1 copy per deck. End of turn requires 1 unspent Mana per Legend on field, or they return to your deck.
  • Fusion (F): Costs 5 Mana to fuse. Requires both component troops on field. Fused troops return to deck. Max 3 total Fusions.
