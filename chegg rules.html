📜 Rules Overview

  • Core Rule: Standard Chegg rules apply. The Villager is replaced by a Crown (movement remains unchanged).
  • Legend (L): Max 1 copy per deck. End of turn requires 1 unspent Mana per Legend on field, or they return to your deck.
  • Fusion (F): Costs 5 Mana to fuse. Requires both component troops on field. Fused troops return to deck. Max 3 total Fusion cards per deck.
  • Grid Notations: x = Standard tile range | o = Unit | Numbers (2, 4) = Range depth (hitting squares 1 up to N) | I = Infinite range.
